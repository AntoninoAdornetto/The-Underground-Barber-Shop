Biagio's Vision

Biagio has a strong aim for cutting and styling men's hair. Since 2001 he has welcomed clients into his shop and consulted with them on desired styles. He uses a plethora of different techniques but is well known across the treasure valley for having some of the best scissor work. It’s very easy for barbers to master a fade with clippers but having experience with scissor work is essential for being a well versed Barber. The services that Biagio offers are listed below:

  • Men's Hair Cut
  • Shampoo & Conditioner
  • Comedy Show
  • All of this for only $30

The Underground
Barbershop

If you recall what downtown Boise looked like 20 years ago, including the garage this shop is located in, you wouldn’t remember The Underground as a barbershop, you would remember it as a storage unit for cleaning supplies. Since then it’s transformed into the smallest downtown store front and is truly a unique establishment where many people come each day to experience the services Biagio offers. The shop holds a special place in the downtown small business community and even the KTVB did a segment on the shop. The link to the video is below alongside the link to Biagio’s FaceBook.
